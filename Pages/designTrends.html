Importance of Packaging Design

Packaging design plays a very important role in branding products in the retail environment. Designs are a recognition signal, helping consumers quickly recognise a brand or product. A good design is distinctive, and helps to convey the values that differentiates a brand.

How design impacts on how consumers think and feel about your brand


There are many elements of packaging that hold the power to attract consumers. Elements like colors, images, typography, print styles and overall design can attract and evoke subliminal emotions and connections.
